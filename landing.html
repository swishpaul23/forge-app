FAQ

Straight
answers.

What happens if I miss a day?
+
Your streak resets and the Wall records it honestly. You can invoke a one-time Recovery per challenge — your streak pauses instead of resetting, and the miss is marked ◈ on the Wall. Visible, not erased. Use it once, use it wisely.
Can I build my own challenge?
+
Yes. You define the name, duration (7 to 365 days), and every daily task. No template needed. The custom challenge is fully supported everywhere in the app — Wall, AI insights, partners, all of it.
Is my data private?
+
Your data is stored securely in Supabase. It's yours. Partners only see your challenge name, streak, and whether you've logged today. Nothing else is shared unless you explicitly message them.
What is a Scaled Day?
+
A scaled day lets you complete a reduced set of tasks (your Non-Negotiables) when life genuinely gets in the way. Your streak stays intact but counts at 50% consistency. Max 2 scaled days per week — it's a lifeline, not a loophole.
What is TALOS?
+
TALOS is Forge's autonomous AI agent — currently in development. It will read your last 7 days of data and tell you exactly what's slipping and what to focus on. No cheerleading. Just honest signal. Available soon on the Forged plan.
Can I run multiple challenges at once?
+
Yes — one main challenge plus up to three secondaries (Forged plan). Secondaries must end within the main challenge's timeframe. All tasks from all challenges appear in your daily check-in. Stack disciplines, not excuses.
